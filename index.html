About Me

About

"The user’s comes first."

Hi, my name is Isaiah Adebayo Akinremi and I’m a Product Designer! Designing and creating are something that comes naturally to me. It all started when I was 10 years old, when my dad opened a computer training center, there was a drink called "Limca" then, I picked up the cover from the floor and redesign their logo using MS PowerPoint. It was the coolest thing ever.

I completed my Higher National Diploma in Computer Engineering with plans of going into IT fully, which I worked for an Engineering and Automation company as an IT Support Engineer for 2years, however, my passion for design grew stronger and I decided to take the plunge and follow the path to designing. I decided to become a Product Designer because I love using design to solve problems. It’s fascinating to me how combining empathy and creativity can lead to such impactful user-centered products. I have spent the past 8 months designing and collaborating, straightforward yet effective user experiences through websites for clients. I have also taken much time through education and consistent practice to equip myself with the relevant design skills, expertise, and modern software knowledge required to thrive in this field.
